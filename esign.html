1. Laptop/PC (Adobe Acrobat Reader DC)
Guideline
To sign a PDF document, you can type, draw, or upload an image of your handwritten signature. You can also add text, such as your name, company, title, or the date. When you save the document, the signature and text become part of the PDF.
Step 1 : Open the PDF document that you want to sign with Adobe Acrobat Reader DC.

Step 2 : Click the Sign icon.

Step 3: Choose Add Signature.

Step 4: Create Signature and Apply.
Option 1: Draw. Draw your signature in the field using your mouse/trackpad.

Option 2: Image. Browse, select and upload an image of your signature.

Tick Save Signature box. When this box is selected, the added signature is saved in Adobe Acrobat Reader DC for reuse.
Click Apply , and then place the Signature in the PDF where required. Resize and move the signature by clicking on the Signature to select it.

Step 5: Save PDF to finalize the signed document.
