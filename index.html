Information




1. Configure VPN at first time

Step Screenshot
Open FortiClient VPN from Start menu
Click Configure VPN
Enter as below:
  • Conection Name: HOIANA VPN
  • Remote Gateway: vpn201.hoiana.com
  • Customize Port: 9443
Enter Username & Password, then click Connect
You can have successful login with VPN Connected

Remote Support

Step Screenshot
Press/Click Windows button and type Quick Assist.
Input 6 digits is provided by IT Supporter and press Share screen button.
Waiting for IT Supporter’s response, depended on internet speed, may up to 60s.
Double check again the IT Support name and press Allow button.
Close everything confidentially before click Allow.
The support session is established when Screen sharing on is display. Press x to terminate any times.
